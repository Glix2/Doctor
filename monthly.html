Monthly Maintenance Service

Tired of trying to keep your hot tub clean?

Having problems upkeeping your tub? Look no further!

Our maintenance services include cleaning, water chemistry adjustments, filter cleanings, and more. Our team of experts will work with you to create a maintenance schedule that fits your needs and budget. We will also provide you with the necessary guidelines and tips to keep your hot tub in top condition between maintenance visits, our programs also include discounted or even free diagnosis of any issues.

Regular maintenance keeps your hot tub clean and sanitized, prevents the growth of bacteria and other microorganisms, and keeps the water safe to use. It also holds the water chemistry in balance to keep your equipment running efficiently, this helps extend the life of your hot tub and reduce your energy costs.

Monthly Maintenance services performed on a monthly basis or bi-monthly includes, but are not limited to:

  • Assure equipment is runnning properly.
  • Clean cover outside and inside.
  • Underwater scrub of seats, jets, suction fittings and floor.
  • Cleaning of filter and headrests.
  • Underwater vacuum of debris including small particles nets can't get!
  • Cycle in of fresh water.
  • Chemical check and adjustment including routine shock.

ANNUAL CHARGE: Call for current rates in your service area and applicable discounts.

*All scheduled services are subject to change as a result of inclement weather.
