About TheSpaDoctor

Since 1996, we have been repairing and refurbishing hot tubs and whirlpool bathtubs in Washington D.C., the Northern Virginia suburbs, and parts of Stafford County. We service and repair all types and brands of hot tubs and spas. We also offer expert mechanical repair of almost all brands of whirlpool bathtubs. In addition to repairs, we offer professional cleaning and maintenance including Platinum Monthly Maintenance Service and Deluxe Chemical Drain and Clean. Let The Spa Doctor be the service professionals you trust to keep your hot tub worry free and its water sparkly clean and inviting. Please call us TODAY and let us know how we can be of service to you.
