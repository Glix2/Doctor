Ask us about adding an Ozonator that can enhance the water quality in your hot tub!

An Ozonator is a device that is added to a hot tub to help sanitize the water using the ozone molecule, Ozonators can improve overall water quality and reduce the use of chemical sanitizers like chlorine. Ozone is a form of oxygen that is created when an electrical current is passed through regular oxygen. When Ozone is used as an oxidizer in spa water, the quality of the water will improve as complications with combined chlorine compounds are eliminated and eye and skin irritations are notably decreased. Maintenance and care are reduced from normal as clogging greases and oils are broken down, preventing filter clogging.
